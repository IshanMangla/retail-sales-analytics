# Markdown

This notebook performs exploratory data analysis (EDA) on a retail sales dataset for a multi-city Indian retail chain.
Objectives:
- Understand overall sales performance and key KPIs.
- Identify top-performing stores and product categories.
- Analyze monthly and weekday/weekend sales patterns.
- Create charts that can be reused in dashboards.



## Key Insights

- Identify which store has the highest total revenue and which is underperforming.
- Compare which product category (Electronics, Clothing, Groceries, Home Decor) is driving most of the sales.
- Highlight seasonality or monthly peaks in revenue.
- Note whether weekends outperform weekdays.
- Suggest focus areas, such as promoting strong categories in weaker stores.
